Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

TheSoftEdit ("we", "us", or "our") operates as an affiliate content curation service. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our use of the Pinterest API and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from visitors to this website. When operating through the Pinterest API, we access only the permissions necessary to publish curated content (Pins) to our own Pinterest business account. We do not collect, store, or process data belonging to other Pinterest users.

2. How We Use the Pinterest API

TheSoftEdit uses the Pinterest API solely to:

  • Publish Pins to our own Pinterest business board
  • Read performance metrics (impressions, saves) for Pins we have posted
  • Manage our own boards and Pin content

We do not use the Pinterest API to access, collect, or store data from any Pinterest user other than our own account.

3. Affiliate Links

Our Pins may contain affiliate links (primarily through the Amazon Associates program). When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. We do not track individual users across sites. Affiliate link click data is aggregated and anonymized.

4. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics scripts, or any tracking technology. Third-party platforms we link to (such as Pinterest and Amazon) may set their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with the following third parties, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Pinterest — policy.pinterest.com/en/privacy-policy
  • Amazon Associates — amazon.com privacy notice

6. Data Retention

We retain only the data necessary to operate our Pinterest posting pipeline (such as post IDs and performance metrics for our own published content). No personal data from third parties is retained.

7. Children's Privacy

TheSoftEdit is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
